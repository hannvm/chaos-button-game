Welcome to the Chaos Button Game

Instructions:

1. Select how many colours you want to play with.

2. Click Generate Colours to randomnly generate your desired number of colours and target colour

3. Each time you click on a button it will turn a different colour.

Your objective is to get them all to match

Oh, in order to make the experience as uncomfortable as possible, the background colour will change too
